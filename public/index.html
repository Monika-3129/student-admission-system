Learnt From Here

Design Principles

Like every major spectacle ever created the very first thing was its idea, how actually should it be constructed or planned and similar was my experience with this project, I started planning the design and positions of the components on a sheet of paper and then proceeded with how could I realize my designs.

User Interface and Experience

The purpose of a project, apart from its core necessity, is that it should give users a feel of being at home, the designs, components, flow of controls, animations, actions, interactivities should never let them feel that this is something alien to them. The Icons should be used accordingly, pages should named properly, appropriate animations should be put in places. All a user wants is a nice experience while they visit any site.

Data Integrity and Privacy

For what it is worth, nothing is fool-proof and 100 percent secure on the internet, there are always some spoofers and prying eyes looking out for any vulnerability in contents over the web and we as developers have the power as well as right to defend our visitors / users from such attacks. For achieving data security and integrity, we use Google's world class infrastructures accoring to our needs.

Simplicity and Robustness

It is clear, as of now, that the best things in the world are just so simple and elegant. What makes one special is its core values, anything created out of pure love and passion is of the highest quality, be it a web page or a plum pudding, without love and 100 % of your effort, nothing is of any worth. This site is built with a great care using simple and basic infrastructure while focussing on user experience and quality content.
