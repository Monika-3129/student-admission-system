About GTC

Ganga Technical Campus is spread over 15 acres of virgin, pollution free and calm environment and is run in two separate buildings with a total built-up area of 2 lakh sq ft. which exceeds the norms laid down by the AICTE. It provides facilities of education and research in various professional programmes at Diploma, UG & PG levels in Engineering, Management and Computer Application under one roof.

What had made this portal possible

Apart from my imagination and a need for paperless admission system below are the pillars of this portal

  • Front-End

    I have used just the basic building blocks of web i.e. Bootstrap consisting of its responsiveness built over HTML, CSS and jS & jQuery so that my portal remains client, server as well as SEO and performance friendly.

  • Back-End

    For all the hosting, database, and authentication needs I have used Firebase as it enables fast, robust and scalable development and deployment.
