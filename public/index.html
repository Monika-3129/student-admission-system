About GITAM

Ganga Institute of Technology and Management (GITAM) is a renowned educational institution in Kablana, Jhajjar district of Haryana, India. Established in 2008 by the Pawan Ganga Educational Society, GITAM has emerged as a leading technical and management education Institute. The Institute is duly approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education) and Affiliated to Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU, Rohtak Graded A+ by NAAC). GITAM is also recognized under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956. GITAM is recognized for its commitment to providing high-quality education and fostering holistic student development. The Institute offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and computer applications. A notable achievement of GITAM is its accreditation with an 'A' grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). This prestigious accreditation is a testament to the Institute's dedication to maintaining exceptional standards in education, infrastructure, faculty, and overall academic performance. The 'A' grade rating reflects the institution's continuous efforts to provide a conducive learning environment and ensure the all-round development of its students. GITAM is Ranked 52nd across India and 17th in North India by Times Engineering Institute Ranking Survey 2024 by Times of India. The B.Tech Electronics and Communication Engineering program at GITAM is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). This accreditation further validates the program's quality and ensures that it meets the NBA standards. The accreditation also serves as a mark of assurance for students and potential employers, indicating that the program provides the necessary skills and knowledge required in the industry. GITAM has highly qualified and experienced faculty dedicated to imparting quality education to students. The faculty members possess expertise in their respective fields and employ innovative teaching methodologies to facilitate effective learning. They emphasize practical knowledge and provide hands-on training through well-equipped laboratories and industry-relevant projects. The Institute's infrastructure is modern and well-maintained. It comprises state-of-the-art laboratories, well-equipped classrooms, a resourceful library, and advanced computing facilities. These facilities enable students to gain practical exposure and develop their technical skills. GITAM also organizes alumni talks, seminars, workshops, and industrial visits to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world applications.

Learnt From Here

Design Principles

Like every major spectacle ever created the very first thing was its idea, how actually should it be constructed or planned and similar was my experience with this project, I started planning the design and positions of the components on a sheet of paper and then proceeded with how could I realize my designs.

User Interface and Experience

The purpose of a project, apart from its core necessity, is that it should give users a feel of being at home, the designs, components, flow of controls, animations, actions, interactivities should never let them feel that this is something alien to them. The Icons should be used accordingly, pages should named properly, appropriate animations should be put in places. All a user wants is a nice experience while they visit any site.

Data Integrity and Privacy

For what it is worth, nothing is fool-proof and 100 percent secure on the internet, there are always some spoofers and prying eyes looking out for any vulnerability in contents over the web and we as developers have the power as well as right to defend our visitors / users from such attacks. For achieving data security and integrity, we use Google's world class infrastructures accoring to our needs.

Simplicity and Robustness

It is clear, as of now, that the best things in the world are just so simple and elegant. What makes one special is its core values, anything created out of pure love and passion is of the highest quality, be it a web page or a plum pudding, without love and 100 % of your effort, nothing is of any worth. This site is built with a great care using simple and basic infrastructure while focussing on user experience and quality content.
